Welcome to Villa Pancho

Located in the heart of Watfords vibrant cafe quarter, Villa Pancho serves authentic mexican cuisine and a wide range of cocktails, beers and wines. Enjoy a vibrant atmosphere on our salsa nights and our unique party scene makes Villa Pancho the ideal venue for your celebration. We source ingredients from many local suppliers to create our traditional dishes aswell as Tex-Mex favourites. Our extensive range of mexican beers and tequilas give you the real flavour of mexico.
